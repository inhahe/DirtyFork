
Upgrade Instructions for Legend of the Red Dragon 4.07


   ° TO UPGRADE TO 4.07 °

   1) Contact GamePort to obtain new registration codes if you
      have a registered game

      NOTE! If you have a registered game, LORD will NOT run until you
      obtain new registration codes!

   2) Unzip LORD.ZIP over your existing LORD directory.

   3) Edit START.BAT to reflect your directories.

   4) Run LORDCFG.EXE
      A) When prompted to re-enter your registration information, do so.

      B) Do a complete reset of the game

      C) Go through the rest of LORDCFG and make sure the game is setup
         the way you want it.

   5) ENJOY THE GAME!
